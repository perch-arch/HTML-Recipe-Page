Chicken Alfredo Pasta

Creamy Chicken Alfredo Pasta is a simple and filling dish made with tender chicken, pasta, garlic, cream, and parmesan cheese. It is a good comfort meal for lunch or dinner and can be prepared without too much stress.

Prep Time

15 minutes


Cook Time

25 minutes


Total Time

40 minutes


Servings

4


Difficulty

Easy to Medium


Ingredients

  • 300g fettuccine or spaghetti pasta
  • 2 boneless chicken breasts, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • ½ teaspoon black pepper
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • Fresh parsley, chopped, for garnish

Cooking Instructions

  1. Put the pasta in salted water according to the package instructions until it is soft but still firm. Drain and set aside.
  2. Season the sliced chicken with salt, black pepper, and Italian seasoning.
  3. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat, then cook the chicken until it is golden brown and fully cooked. Remove from the pan and set aside.
  4. In the same pan, add butter and minced garlic. Stir for about 1 minute until the garlic becomes fragrant.
  5. Pour in the heavy cream and allow it to simmer gently for a few minutes.
  6. Add the grated parmesan cheese and stir until the sauce becomes smooth and creamy.
  7. Add the cooked pasta and chicken back into the pan, then mix until everything is well coated with the Alfredo sauce.
  8. Garnish with chopped parsley and serve warm.

Tips or Notes

  1. Do not boil the cream too much, as this may affect the texture of the sauce.
  2. Add a little pasta water if the sauce becomes too thick.
  3. You can add vegetables like broccoli, mushrooms, or spinach to make the meal more balanced.
  4. For extra flavour, use freshly grated parmesan cheese instead of powdered cheese.
